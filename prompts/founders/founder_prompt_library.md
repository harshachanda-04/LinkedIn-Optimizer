# Founder Prompt Library

## 1. Founder Profile Positioning Prompt

Use this prompt to help a founder present a strong leadership and business story.

Prompt:
"Act as a LinkedIn profile writer for a founder. Rewrite my profile so it communicates vision, market understanding, leadership, growth strategy, and business impact. Make it sound confident, credible, and investor-ready while remaining human and engaging."

## 2. Executive Visibility Prompt

Prompt:
"Create a polished LinkedIn About section for a founder who wants to attract investors, partners, and top talent. Focus on the company mission, growth momentum, leadership philosophy, and key achievements."
