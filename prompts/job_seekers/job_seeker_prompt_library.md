# Job Seeker Prompt Library

## 1. Job Seeker Profile Optimization Prompt

Use this prompt to tailor a profile toward target roles and recruiter searches.

Prompt:
"Act as a LinkedIn profile strategist for a job seeker. Rewrite my headline, About section, and experience bullets to better align with my target role. Emphasize relevant skills, achievements, and keywords that improve recruiter discoverability."

## 2. Career Transition Prompt

Prompt:
"Help me rewrite my LinkedIn profile for a career transition into a new industry. Position my transferable skills, relevant experience, and professional strengths in a way that feels credible and strategic."
