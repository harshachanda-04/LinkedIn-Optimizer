# LinkedIn Optimizer Prompt Pack

This pack includes a curated set of prompts for optimizing a LinkedIn profile.

## 1. Headline Prompt
Use this to create strong headline options.

Prompt:
Write 10 professional LinkedIn headline options for a [role] in [industry]. Focus on value, credibility, and keywords relevant to the role. Keep them concise and persuasive.

## 2. About Section Prompt
Use this to rewrite the About section.

Prompt:
Write a compelling LinkedIn About section for a professional with [years] years of experience in [industry]. Highlight strengths, achievements, and the value they bring to employers or clients. Keep the tone professional and confident.

## 3. Experience Bullet Prompt
Use this to turn responsibilities into achievements.

Prompt:
Rewrite my experience bullets into strong LinkedIn-ready achievement statements. Focus on action, outcomes, and measurable impact. Include relevant keywords and keep each bullet concise.

## 4. Profile Audit Prompt
Use this to review the full profile.

Prompt:
Act as a LinkedIn profile strategist. Review my current profile and suggest improvements to the headline, About section, skills, experience, and overall positioning. Provide a stronger version of each section.

## 5. Recruiter Prompt
Use this to position a recruiter profile.

Prompt:
Rewrite my LinkedIn profile to highlight recruiting expertise, stakeholder management, talent advisory skills, and measurable hiring outcomes. Emphasize credibility and relationship-building.

## 6. Founder Prompt
Use this to position a founder profile.

Prompt:
Rewrite my LinkedIn profile to communicate leadership, vision, business growth, and strategic impact. Make it sound credible, polished, and founder-ready.

## 7. Job Seeker Prompt
Use this to align a profile with target roles.

Prompt:
Rewrite my LinkedIn profile for a target role in [industry]. Emphasize relevant skills, achievements, and keywords that increase recruiter visibility.
