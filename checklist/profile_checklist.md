# LinkedIn Profile Optimization Checklist

Use this checklist before publishing your profile.

## Essentials
- [ ] Headline clearly states your role and value
- [ ] Profile photo is professional and recent
- [ ] Banner image is relevant and polished
- [ ] About section is specific, not generic
- [ ] Experience section includes achievements, not just duties
- [ ] Skills section includes relevant keywords
- [ ] Featured section highlights your best work

## Strength signals
- [ ] You include measurable results where possible
- [ ] Your content uses clear language and strong verbs
- [ ] Your profile is tailored to your target audience
- [ ] Your profile feels consistent across headline, About, and experience

## Final review
- [ ] Spelling and grammar are correct
- [ ] The profile sounds confident and professional
- [ ] It is easy to understand within 10 seconds
